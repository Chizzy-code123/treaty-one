Today, the government provides reserves for indigenous people just like they promised. There are self-governed education programs and Indigenous peoples history is being taught around schools. At the site of the former Kapyong Barracks (now named Naawi-Oodena), the seven treaty one nations are turning that urban reserve into a cultural and economic hub for the city of Winnipeg where people can go and celebrate indigenous culture. The project started in April 2024.
